Finch ¬∑ Brand Design

A financial app tailored to Gen Z. It aims to empower young people to achieve financial independence and manage their money responsibly. This project's design outcomes include a brand guide, website, and app.

Role: Visual Designer, Branding
Technique: Figma, Procreate, Illustrator, Photoshop
Medium: Digital
Duration: 15 weeks
Collaborator: None

Problem to Solve:

Many Gen Z individuals lack financial literacy and confidence. They often turn to friends and family for guidance, highlighting the need for a trusted resource tailored to their generation.

Insight:

Our research revealed a surge in Gen Z's online engagement with aesthetics like cottagecore, dreamcore, and light academia. These aesthetics, defined by Aesthetics Wiki, showcase a desire for both mild adventure and a comfortable daily life. Recognizing the power of aesthetics to connect and inspire, Finch will leverage this insight to create a unique approach to financial education.
