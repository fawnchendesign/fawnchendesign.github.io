Pierce The Veil

2024 ¬∑ SEATTLE CENTRAL COLLEGE
Role: Visual Designer
Technique: Indesign, Procreate
Medium: Printing Material
Duration: 2 weeks
Collaborator: None

This poster is designed to evoke a sense of longing in Pierce the Veil's most dedicated fans, the "Always Fans," prompting them to purchase a piece of the band's legacy. The artwork channels the feeling of being stuck in a harsh reality, yearning for escape. It achieves this mood through a combination of gritty textures, a relatable theme of breaking free from a difficult situation, and a touch of nostalgia. This emotional resonance is what makes the poster truly connect with Pierce the Veil's fanbase. It captures the frustration of being trapped but also offers a glimmer of hope with abstract shapes hinting at a potential way out.
