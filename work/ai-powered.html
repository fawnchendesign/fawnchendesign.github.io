Runing Horse

"Spirit: Stallion of the Cimarron" has held a special place in my heart since I was eight years old. The film's portrayal of untamed freedom, especially the horses galloping across vast grasslands, always evokes a deep emotional response in me. It even brings tears to my eyes when the music swells.
‍
This project started with a sketch of Spirit's powerful form in Procreate Dream.  To capture the boundless spirit of the film, I used Midjourney to generate a breathtaking background that complements the horse's energy and grace.

Role: Prompt Designer
Technique: Procreate, Midjourney
Medium: Digital
Duration: 11 weeks

Beyond the Dream Veil

AI-Generated Film

Role: Prompt Designer
Technique: PixVerse, Midjourney, Photoshop
Medium: Digital
Duration: 11 weeks

This is an experimental film project pushing the boundaries of storytelling. Utilizing the power of generative AI, it explores a man's dreamlike journey of self-discovery, where he confronts surreal encounters - a fish, Buddha, a transforming koi - that prompt him to question reality and his own limitations. Each encounter becomes a stepping stone on his path to uncovering hidden potential and possibilities beyond the ordinary.

To achieve a unique and dreamlike aesthetic, I leverage Midjourney to generate stunning visuals reminiscent of Chinese ink painting, perfectly capturing the dreamlike quality of the narrative. PixVerse then allows me to selectively animate specific elements. This two-step process ensures a cohesive visual experience that maintains the desired aesthetic.
